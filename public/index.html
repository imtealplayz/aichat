What is Bou?

Bou is an AI-powered chat assistant designed to answer questions, explain concepts, and help with everyday tasks clearly and concisely.

How it works

This app integrates external AI models to generate responses. Messages are sent securely to the backend — your API key is never exposed to the browser.

Privacy

Bou does not store conversations on any server. Chats are saved only on your device using localStorage. See the Info page for details.

How Bou saves chats

Everything you need to know about how your data is stored and handled.

Stored on your device only

Bou saves your chat history using localStorage — a feature built into your browser. Your conversations never leave your device. No data is sent to any server, database, or third party for storage purposes.

How chat history works

Bou keeps a maximum of 5 chats at a time. When you start a 6th chat, the oldest one is automatically removed. Each chat is named automatically by the AI based on your first message.

What gets cleared

Your chat history will be lost if you clear your browser's site data or use private/incognito mode. Chats are also specific to the browser and device you're using — they won't sync across devices.

What we don't collect

Bou does not collect your name, email, IP address, or any personal information. Your messages are sent to the AI API only to generate a response and are not stored or logged by us.

How to clear your chats

You can delete individual chats using the ✕ button next to each chat in the sidebar. To clear everything, go to your browser settings and clear site data for this website.
