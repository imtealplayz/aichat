Image Generation

How BouAI generates images and what you should know.

Images are saved as URLs — not files

Generated images are stored as links to Pollinations.AI. Download any image you want to keep permanently.

How to generate

Say "generate an image of a forest at night" or "draw a futuristic city". BouAI detects your intent automatically.

Powered by Pollinations.AI

Free image generation, no key needed. Takes 3–8 seconds. Click any image to zoom. Use Regenerate for a new version.

Downloading

Every image has a Download button that saves directly to your device as PNG — no new tab.

What BouAI won't generate

Violence, nudity, real identifiable people, or offensive content will be refused.

What BouAI knows about you

Review, edit, or delete anything BouAI has learned from your conversations.

How BouAI works

Privacy, data storage, and feature details.

Guest mode

Without an account, chats are saved on your device only (up to 5). Memory is local. Image upload is not available.

Signed in

With an account: up to 10 chats synced across devices, memory synced to cloud, image upload enabled, better memory recall.

What we collect

Only your email (for auth) and your chat/memory data — stored in Supabase. We never sell or share your data.

Deleting your data

Delete individual chats with ✕ in the sidebar. Clear all memory from the Memory page. Contact Teal on Discord to delete your account.
