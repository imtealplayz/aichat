Image Generation

How BouAI generates and saves images — and what you need to know.

Images are saved as URLs — not files

Generated images are stored as links to Pollinations.AI's servers. Download any image you want to keep permanently using the Download button.

How to generate an image

Say "generate an image of a forest at night" or "draw a futuristic city". BouAI detects your intent and generates automatically. If unclear, it will ask first.

Powered by Pollinations.AI

Free image generation, no key needed. Takes 3–8 seconds per image. Click an image to zoom in. Use the Regenerate button for a new version.

Downloading images

Every image has a Download button that saves it directly to your device as a PNG — no new tab required.

What BouAI won't generate

Requests involving violence, nudity, real identifiable people, or offensive content will be refused.

How BouAI works

Privacy, data storage, and how everything fits together.

Stored on your device only

BouAI uses localStorage. Conversations never leave your device and are not sent anywhere for storage.

Chat history limit

BouAI keeps a maximum of 5 chats. When you start a 6th, the oldest is removed. Each chat is named by AI based on your first message.

What we don't collect

No name, email, IP, or personal info is ever collected. Messages are sent to AI APIs only to generate responses — not stored or logged by us.
