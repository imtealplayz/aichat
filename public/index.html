What is Bou?

An AI-powered assistant that answers questions, explains concepts, writes code, and generates images — clearly and concisely.

How it works

Messages go to a secure backend that queries an external AI API. Your API key is never exposed to the browser.

Image Generation

How Bou generates and saves images — and what you need to know.

Images are saved as URLs — not files

Generated images are stored as links to Pollinations.AI's servers, not as actual image files on your device. If their servers remove or change the image, it may no longer load. Download any image you want to keep permanently.

How to generate an image

Just ask naturally — "generate an image of a forest at night" or "draw a futuristic city". Bou will detect your intent and generate the image automatically. If your intent is unclear, Bou will ask first.

Powered by Pollinations.AI

Images are generated using Pollinations.AI — a free, no-key-required AI image service. It typically takes 3–8 seconds per image. Occasionally longer during peak hours.

Downloading images

Every generated image has a Download button. Click it to save the image to your device as a PNG file. We strongly recommend downloading any image you want to keep, as saved URLs may expire.

What Bou won't generate

Bou will refuse image requests that involve violence, nudity, real identifiable people, or offensive content. The AI will tell you if a request is not allowed.

Where images are saved

Image URLs are saved in your browser's localStorage alongside your chat history. They are stored on your device only and are never sent to our servers. Clearing site data will remove them.

How Bou saves chats

Everything about how your data is stored and handled.

Stored on your device only

Bou uses localStorage — built into your browser. Conversations never leave your device and are not sent anywhere for storage.

Chat history limit

Bou keeps a maximum of 5 chats. When you start a 6th, the oldest is removed. Each chat is named by the AI based on your first message.

What gets cleared

History is lost if you clear browser site data or use incognito mode. Chats don't sync across devices or browsers.

What we don't collect

No name, email, IP, or personal info is ever collected. Messages are sent to the AI API only to generate a response — not stored or logged by us.

How to clear chats

Click ✕ next to any chat in the sidebar. To clear everything, clear site data for this site in your browser settings.
